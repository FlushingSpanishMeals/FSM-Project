About us

Who We Are

Flushing Spanish meals is a small business company based in Flushing Queens, which caters Spanish food. From the comfort of our home, we select and cook just a few of the most traditional Spanish dishes from the Caribbean, and some Central and South American backgrounds cuisine. Empanadas are always a big hit in most Spanish countries because they are a simple, fulfilling and comfortable snack served on any occasion. Roasted Pernil, roasted or stew chicken with rice & pigeon peas is a priority for numerous Spanish parties and gatherings. We focus on some of the most traditional dishes that most Spanish people have good memories of enjoying at a reunion. We want to ensure that whether is a corporate lunch or small gathering, a birthday, or just a single order for yourself, to provide you with a fresh meal that tastes as if you were back in your country.
